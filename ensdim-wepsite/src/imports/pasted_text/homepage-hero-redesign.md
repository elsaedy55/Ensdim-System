ANIMATION CONCEPT
-----------------------------------

Create a premium abstract hero animation inspired by the human nervous system.

The animation should be made of fine, elegant, interconnected lines and signal paths.

The visual meaning:
ENSDIM is the intelligent system that understands human and operational complexity, then organizes it into clarity.

Animation sequence:

Phase 1 — Human signal emergence:
Thin abstract lines begin to appear softly.
They feel organic, intelligent, and alive.
They resemble a stylized nervous system or neural pathways, but not a medical illustration.

Phase 2 — Complexity and chaos:
The lines multiply and become dense.
They overlap, cross, and become highly tangled.
The visual should feel like operational chaos, scattered workflows, human behavior, data signals, and business complexity.

Phase 3 — Listening intelligence:
Subtle pulses move through the lines.
The system appears to be listening, understanding, and reading the signals.
Movement should feel calm, precise, and intelligent, not chaotic or aggressive.

Phase 4 — Organization:
The tangled lines begin to reorganize.
Some lines fade away.
Some lines align into clearer paths.
The network becomes more structured and intentional.
The chaos gradually turns into order.

Phase 5 — ENSDIM formation:
The organized lines subtly form the word:
ENSDIM

The final wordmark should feel clean, premium, stable, and intelligent.
The final reveal should communicate:
Chaos → Listening → Understanding → Structure → ENSDIM

The first two letters “EN” can receive a subtle red accent to support brand pronunciation.
The rest of the word should feel refined and premium.
