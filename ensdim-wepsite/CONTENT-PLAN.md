## CONTENT PRODUCTION GUIDELINES

### For SEO (Google ranking)
- Minimum 1,200 words per article
- Use primary keyword in: H1, first paragraph, one H2, conclusion
- Add 3-5 internal links per article
- Add structured data (BlogPosting schema) on each article
- Optimize images with keyword-rich alt text

### For GEO (AI search appearance)
- Include a direct "What is X" answer in the first 100 words
- Use numbered lists and headers throughout
- Include specific statistics and named entities
- Write in both English and Arabic for full MENA coverage
- Keep sentences factual, not promotional

### Publishing Cadence
- **Target:** 4 articles per month
- **Priority order:** CRM articles first (highest commercial intent), then AI articles, then operations
- **Bilingual:** Publish EN version first, AR version within 2 weeks
